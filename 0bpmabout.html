0BPM
zero beats per minute
Zero beats per minute is the name of a fiction based on “The State of Thigs” by Wim Wenders. In this fictional world, time goes by in a different way. A slower way.

It has been many years since the last person died. No one remembers death, at least death within humans. Time is perceived through other things, exterior to us. Objects. They have a life, they appear and disappear, they are usefull and then useless. They make us perceive time through their degradation or loss of function. Everything is connected – people and innanimate objects - and loss is now a different subject, since time influences how we perceive it.
Project developed within the scope of the subject Communication Design VI, Communication Design Degree (2019-20), Faculty of Fine Arts of Lisbon.
